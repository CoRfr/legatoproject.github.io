Become a Partner

Legato partners are members of a worldwide community. Here's some of the things our community does:

  • participate and contribute to the Legato Forum.
  • provide content for Legato users (e.g., application notes, Q&A).
  • provide feedback for Legato improvements.
  • add libraries and develop innotivative IoT features for commercial use.
  • create Legato apps or feature-rich prototypes.
  • commit code on legato.io GitHub (framework modifications or code samples).
  • port technology and libraries to Legato, include innovative IoT features, interest 3rd parties.

Recognize you and your company here? You're a candidate to become a Legato Partner!

Benefit from increased visiblility and access new markets. Co-market with other Legato partners.
