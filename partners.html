Become a partner

Legato™ partners technically contribute to the Legato embedded platform for IoT design. We have simple users who regularly provide feedback and submit practical improvement suggestions, and major contributors who develop value-added features helping build the IoT vision of tomorrow.

As part of our ecosystem, partners increase visibility and get access to new markets.

L&T Technology Services established the first Legato™ Lab, a center of excellence designed to support worldwide customers developing their IoT applications. L&T expertise is covers Legato application architecture and design, embedded Linux distribution customization and training.

Dunasys uses Legato on Sierra Wireless modules and was the first company to port the open source framework on top of other application processors. Dunasys expertise is integration for in-vehicle systems including CAN and connectivity (cellular, Wifi, BT).

Neusoft® ports their Dead Reckoning library on top of Legato to improve positioning accuracy and rebuild position in case of signal loss. Neusoft expertise is integration for automotive navigation and positioning service.

Viveris builds an application collecting CAN messages, routing data at Legato application level through Linux drivers, and sending data to a cloud using the AMQP protocol. Viveris expertise is embedded systems and network for automotive and industrial markets.

Sierra Wireless initiated the Legato Application Framework open source project to support IoT market growth. Sierra Wireless expertise is in wireless module design, development environment for embedded systems, cloud and connectivity services.
